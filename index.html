About Our Council

Representing Student Innovation

The Student Council at Bahir Dar University STEM Incubation Center serves as the voice of the student body, working tirelessly to improve student life and create positive change within our innovative community.

We organize events, advocate for student needs, and foster a sense of belonging among all students in the STEM Incubation Center. Our council members are elected by their peers and represent diverse perspectives from across all programs.

Through collaboration with university administration, faculty, and the broader STEM community, we strive to create an inclusive environment where every student feels heard, valued, and supported in their innovation journey.
